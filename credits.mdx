## What credits are

**Credits** are the in-app balance used for **outgoing voice minutes** and **SMS** that your automation runs. Think of them as a prepaid wallet dedicated to telephony actions, separate from your ad spend in Meta or other ad platforms.

## Where to look

Open **Credits** (`Kredyty`) in the main left menu. You will see:

- **Current balance** — what is available now for calls and SMS according to your project’s rules.
- **History** — when credits were added or spent, so finance and operations can reconcile usage.

## Top-ups

If your organization enabled self-service top-up, the same screen shows how to add credits. Otherwise purchases go through your **account manager** or internal billing process.

## Practical tips

- Watch balance **before** large sends or call bursts so automations do not stop halfway.
- **Credits are account-wide** in the menu view; each campaign still consumes from the same pool when it runs calls or SMS.
