The list of every number bought on the account, shared across all campaigns.

<img src="/images/en/phone-numbers-my.png" alt="Phone numbers, My numbers tab" />

At the top there's a summary: how many numbers you have, the monthly cost, how many are unassigned and the average cost per number. The table shows each number with its type, status, assigned campaign and monthly cost.

## Buying a number

Open **Buy a number** to see what's available, filtered by type and country. Each one shows its monthly cost. **Buy** adds it to your pool and charges the first month straight away. A new number stays "unassigned" until you pick it in a campaign, see [Phone number](/en/campaigns/phone-number).

<img src="/images/en/phone-numbers-buy.png" alt="Buy a number blocked until company verification is complete" />

<Warning>
Buying a number requires a [verified company (KYB)](/en/account-settings#verification). Until verification is done, this screen just points you to it.

You can still build and test a whole campaign without it, the browser test call under **Test agent** works with no verification at all.
</Warning>

<Note>
A number serves only one campaign at a time. To use it elsewhere, disconnect it from the current one first. The monthly cost is fixed and doesn't depend on how many calls you make.
</Note>
