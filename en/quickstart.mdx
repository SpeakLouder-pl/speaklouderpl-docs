---
title: Quick start
description: What to do in the first 15 minutes.
---

## 1. Log in

Open [app.speaklouder.pl](https://app.speaklouder.pl) and sign in.

## 2. Open campaigns

Go to **Moje kampanie** and create a campaign or open an existing one.

<img src="/images/app-moje-kampanie.png" alt="My campaigns page" />

## 3. Set lead source

In campaign menu, open **Formularze** or **Źródło**.

- **Formularze** if leads come from ad forms.
- **Źródło** if leads come from CSV, webhook, or external flow.

## 4. Set voice and number

Open **Agent** and **Numer telefonu**.

- In **Agent**, set how calls should sound.
- In **Numer telefonu**, verify the number used by automation.

## 5. Set workflow

Open **Automatyzacja** and check step order and delays.

<img src="/images/app-kampania-automatyzacja.png" alt="Automation page in campaign" />

## 6. Monitor leads

Open **Leady** to see results and next actions.
