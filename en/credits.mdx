Credits are the balance used for calls and SMS.

<img src="/images/app-kredyty.png" alt="Credits screen with balance and usage" />

## What you can do here

- check current balance,
- review usage history,
- verify transaction list,
- check pricing section in app.

## Practical tip

Before large campaigns, check credits first so automation can run without pause.
