---

Use this integration when leads come from Meta forms.

<img src="/images/app-integracje.png" alt="Integrations page" />

## Steps

1. Open **Integracje**.
2. Click Meta integration card.
3. Complete Meta login and permissions.
4. Back in campaign, choose form in **Formularze**.

## Result

New leads from selected form can appear automatically in your campaign.
