## In short

1. **A lead arrives in the campaign** — from a CSV file, a webhook, Shopify, Meta Lead Ads or added by hand.
2. **It shows up in the CRM** with the status "New".
3. **The workflow decides when to call** — based on the schedule and the sequence of steps.
4. **The agent calls and talks** in the voice and language you chose, following your instructions.
5. **The lead gets an outcome and a follow-up** — qualified / disqualified / no contact — and the agent can send an SMS or an email.

## Where to check progress

- **[Leads](/en/campaigns/leads)** — the status and history of every contact.
- **[Activity → Calls](/en/campaigns/activity)** — the recording, transcript and AI summary of each conversation.
- **[Activity → Action log](/en/campaigns/activity)** — everything that happened in the campaign, in order.

<Note>
You only pay for **answered** calls. Unanswered attempts and voicemail are free — see [pricing](/en/wallet).
</Note>
