title: "Leads"
description: "The CRM list of every contact in the campaign, and the detail card for a single lead."
---

**Leads** is the CRM for one campaign: every contact that came in, what the agent did with them and how each conversation ended. Where those contacts come from is set up separately, in [Source](/en/campaigns/source).

## The list

<img src="/images/en/crm-list.png" alt="CRM lead list with statuses, calls and last activity" />

Each row is one lead: name, **status**, number of calls, when something last happened and when the lead was added. Click a row to open the lead card.

### Statuses

- **New**: in the campaign, not called yet.
- **In progress**: the sequence has started, the agent hasn't reached a verdict yet.
- **Qualified**: the agent decided the lead is interested.
- **Disqualified**: the conversation happened, but there was no interest.
- **No contact**: nobody picked up.

### Finding leads

- **Search**: by name, email or phone number.
- **Filter**: narrow the list down to one or more statuses.
- **Sort**: by lead, number of calls, last activity or date added. Click a column header to flip the direction.

### Working with several leads at once

The checkbox on the left of each row (and the one in the header, which selects everything) lets you act on a group of leads at once.

**Export** downloads the list as a file, so you can hand it to a salesperson or move it into another system.

## The lead card

<img src="/images/en/lead-card-dashboard.png" alt="Lead card, Dashboard tab with sequence, notes and form data" />

The left-hand column holds everything about the person: status (click it to change it by hand), quick actions (**Note**, **Email**, **Call**, **More**), plus tags, contact details and **Lead interest**.

<Note>
**Lead interest** is a 0–100 score the AI assigns based on what was actually said in the calls. It stays empty until the first conversation happens, it isn't a field you fill in yourself.
</Note>

In the middle there are three tabs:

- **Dashboard**: the campaign sequence for this lead (what has run, what is still scheduled), your notes, and **Form data**: everything the source sent along with the contact, including your own custom fields.
- **Notes**: the full history of notes on this lead.
- **Sent emails**: every follow-up email that went out.

On the right, **Stats** counts calls, SMS, emails and answered calls for this lead, and **Recent actions** lists what happened most recently.

<Tip>
**Form data** is the fastest way to check that your source is sending what you think it is. If a custom field is missing here, it never reached SpeakLouder, fix it in [Source](/en/campaigns/source).
</Tip>
