This is the first tab you land on inside a campaign, a list of everything to do before the agent starts calling. Each item is a card that links straight to the right screen.

<img src="/images/en/campaign-get-started.png" alt="Get started checklist inside a campaign" />

In order: **set up the agent**, **set up the workflow**, **test the agent** (a browser conversation, free of charge), **connect a lead source**, **assign a phone number**, and finally **launch the campaign**.

Further down there are shortcuts to help: a free consultation with an expert and this documentation.

<Note>
The checklist stays put even after everything is configured, treat it as a reference. For day-to-day work, use the **Dashboard** tab in the campaign menu.
</Note>
