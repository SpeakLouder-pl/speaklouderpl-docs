**Campaigns** lists every campaign on the account, whatever its type or status.

<img src="/images/en/campaigns-list.png" alt="Campaign list with summary tiles and table" />

At the top you get a quick summary: how many campaigns you have, calls made, total leads and average conversion. Each row in the table is one campaign, its **type** (where the leads come from), its **status** and its lead count. Click **Details** to go into it.

## Statuses

- **Active**: the agent is calling according to the workflow.
- **Paused**: the campaign exists, but the agent isn't calling. Switch it back on in [Campaign settings](/en/campaigns/settings).

## New campaign

The **New campaign** button (here and at the top of the menu) starts the creation flow, see [Your first campaign](/en/quickstart).

<Tip>
Once you're inside a campaign, you can jump to another one from the dropdown in the top-left corner, no need to come back to this page.
</Tip>
