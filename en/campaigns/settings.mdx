Settings that apply to this one campaign only (account-wide settings live in [Account settings](/en/account-settings)).

<img src="/images/en/campaign-settings.png" alt="Campaign settings with notifications and danger zone" />

## General

The campaign name, the same one you see in the list and in the campaign switcher.

## Notifications

Email alerts about what's happening in the campaign:

- **New lead**: when a new contact arrives.
- **New call**: after every completed call. Flip **Only send if qualified** on to cut this down to the calls that matter.

Recipients go in **Sales reps' email**, up to 3 addresses.

## Danger zone

- **Pause / Resume campaign**: stops or restarts the agent's calling without touching any data.
- **Delete campaign**: permanently removes the campaign and everything in it.

<Warning>
Deleting a campaign is irreversible and takes its leads, calls and history with it. If you only want the agent to stop calling, use **Pause** instead.
</Warning>
