**Activity** has two tabs: **Calls** and **Action log**.

## Calls

The list of conversations is on the left, the details of the selected one on the right.

<img src="/images/en/activity-calls.png" alt="Calls with the conversation list, AI summary and transcript" />

Filter with the **All / Qualified / Not qualified** tabs, the search box, or the sort button. For the selected call you get:

- the lead, the phone number and the **date**,
- direction (**Outbound** / **Inbound**), the outcome (**Answered** / no answer) and the duration,
- the **interest score** the AI gave the lead,
- **Summary**: the AI's recap of the conversation,
- **Transcript**: the full exchange as agent and customer bubbles,
- **Play recording**, when a recording is available.

**Open lead** jumps straight to that person's [lead card](/en/campaigns/leads#the-lead-card).

<Note>
Only **answered** conversations show up here. Unanswered attempts and voicemail aren't listed, you'll find those in the Action log.
</Note>

## Action log

<img src="/images/en/activity-action-log.png" alt="Action log with calls and SMS in chronological order" />

Everything that happened in the campaign, newest first: calls, SMS and emails, each with its outcome (`answered`, `No answer`, `sent`) and a timestamp. The date picker in the top-right narrows the view to a single day, and clicking a name opens that lead's card.

<Tip>
The Action log is the quickest answer to "what did the campaign actually do yesterday?", no clicking through individual leads required.
</Tip>
