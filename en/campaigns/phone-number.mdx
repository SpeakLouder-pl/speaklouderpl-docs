This tab shows which number the agent uses for this campaign.

<img src="/images/en/campaign-phone-empty.png" alt="Phone number tab with no number assigned" />

If no number is assigned yet, click **Choose number** and pick one of the numbers on your account. If none are free, buy a new one first — see [Phone numbers](/en/phone-numbers).

Once a number is assigned you get two actions: **Change number** (pick a different one from your pool) and **Disconnect** (the campaign is left without a number and stops calling). **Manage numbers** takes you to the shared list of all your numbers.

<Note>
A number belongs to the **account**, not to a campaign. You can disconnect and reassign it at any time, but it can only serve one campaign at a time.
</Note>
