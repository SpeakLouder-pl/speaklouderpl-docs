SpeakLouder runs on a **wallet** — one prepaid balance in USD for the whole account, under **Settings → Wallet**.

<img src="/images/en/settings-wallet.png" alt="Wallet tab with balance and top-up slider" />

At the top you see your **wallet balance** and your **average monthly usage** over the last 30 days, which helps you judge when to top up again.

## Pricing

<img src="/images/en/settings-pricing.png" alt="Pricing tab listing the cost of each action" />

| Action | Cost |
| --- | --- |
| Completed (answered) call | **$2** per answered call |
| Missed call / voicemail | free |
| SMS | $0.10 per SMS |
| Follow-up email | free |
| Phone number | monthly, depends on the number |

The live prices are always in **Settings → Pricing**.

## Topping up

Under **Top up your wallet** you pick an amount with the slider or a preset button. The more you add at once, the bigger the **bonus**:

| Top-up | Bonus |
| --- | --- |
| $350+ | +5% |
| $1,000+ | +10% |
| $2,000+ | +15% |

The minimum is $50 and the maximum $2,500 in one go (need more? there's a "Talk to us" link). Payment goes through Stripe and the balance updates automatically. It's a one-off payment, not a subscription.

<Note>
You only pay for calls that are **answered**. A phone number is billed separately as a fixed monthly cost, charged as soon as you buy it.
</Note>
