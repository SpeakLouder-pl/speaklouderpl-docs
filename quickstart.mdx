## Before you start

You need:

- A **browser** on a computer (the product is a normal website).
- An **account** created by you or your administrator.

The live app opens at [app.speaklouder.pl](https://app.speaklouder.pl). Bookmarks should point there.

## Sign in and security

1. Open the app link you were given.
2. Sign in with **email and password** or the method your organization enabled.
3. If the product asks for **extra verification** (for example a code from an authenticator app), complete that step. This protects your leads and phone usage.

## After login: three places to know

| Area in the app | What it is for |
| ---------------- | -------------- |
| **Overview** (home) | Snapshot of your campaigns and a button to create a new one. |
| **My campaigns** | List of all campaigns; open one to work inside it. |
| **Integrations** | Connect external accounts (Meta, Gmail, Make) once for your whole login. |

## Open your first campaign

1. Go to **My campaigns**.
2. Click **New campaign** (or open an existing one).
3. Inside a campaign you will see a **left menu** with steps such as **Dashboard**, **Forms** or **Source**, **Agent**, **Phone number**, **Leads**, **Calls**, **Automation**, **Help**, and **Campaign settings**.

Use **Help** inside the campaign for a short in-app guide in Polish that mirrors many topics here.

## Typical next steps

1. **Connect an integration** (if leads come from Meta, Gmail, or Make) under **Integrations** in the main menu — see the Integrations section in this site.
2. In the campaign, set **Forms** or **Source** so the app knows which fields each lead has (name, phone, answers from a form).
3. Configure the **Agent** and **Phone number**, then build **Automation** before turning traffic on.

If something is paused in **Campaign settings**, new leads may not be accepted until you turn it back on.
