## Lead list

Under **Leads** you see a table: search, sort, filters, and each row shows a short summary (stage, last contact, next action). **Details** opens the full lead panel.

<img src="/images/app-leady-lista-jan-kowalski.png" alt="Campaign lead list with one test lead and a Details button" />

## Header of the lead panel

At the top you have:

- The **lead name** (or whatever was stored from the form / webhook).
- A **source** line under the title (how the lead arrived).
- **Back** — returns to the lead list for this campaign.
- **Change status** — manually set pipeline outcome (qualified, not interested, no contact) or **mark as new** to restart the scenario from the beginning. A manual change **cancels scheduled SMS and calls** for this lead (the menu explains this).
- **Delete** — permanently removes the lead, history, and follow-up schedule.
- **Add note** — opens a dialog; notes are visible only in this campaign context.

## Top row cards

### Contact details

Name, **phone**, and optional **email** — the fields automation and SMS placeholders use.

### Contact status

- **Stage** — where the lead is in the **automation scenario** (phase name).
- **Outcome** — pipeline label (e.g. new, qualified, no contact).
- When the stage is **End**, you also see **Scenario outcome** (how the path finished).
- **SMS sent** / **Call attempts** — counters from workflow summary.
- **Created** — when the record was created.

If parallel calls forced the schedule to shift, a **yellow notice** may appear about a random 5–15 minute delay, with a counter of such events.

### Next steps

Either a **concrete upcoming step** (type, phase, due time) or a message like: no pending steps — the **planned scenario and completed actions** are shown **below** (timeline).

## Further down the same page (no separate screenshot)

Scroll on the same screen to see:

- **Data** — question/answer pairs from the lead form or extra fields (if any).
- **Notes / AI analysis** — team notes and AI-tagged entries, with timestamps and delete where allowed.
- **Scenario timeline** — planned and executed steps (calls, SMS, etc.) in order.
- **Contact attempts** — inbound/outbound call details tied to this lead.

## Account settings (main menu)

Under **Settings** (account level, not a single campaign) you typically have **account email** (often read-only — change via support), **low credit email alerts**, **password reset**, and **account deletion**.
