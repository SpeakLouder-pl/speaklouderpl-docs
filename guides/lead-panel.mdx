Open **Leady**, then click **Szczegóły**.

<img src="/images/app-leady-lista-jan-kowalski.png" alt="Lead list with details button" />

## Top section

At the top you can:

- go back to list,
- change lead status,
- add note,
- delete lead.

<img src="/images/app-lead-panel-gora.png" alt="Top of lead details page" />

## What to check first

- **Stage**, where in workflow this lead is.
- **Outcome**, current business result.
- **Next step**, planned next action.
- **Last contact**, last call or message time.

## Lower sections

Scroll down to see:

- source fields,
- notes,
- workflow timeline,
- call attempts.
