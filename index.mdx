SpeakLouder is a web application for **following up with sales leads by phone and text**. You connect where leads come from (for example ads or a spreadsheet), describe how the voice assistant should sound, and build a simple timeline: who gets called or messaged, and when.

The product interface is mostly in **Polish**; this help center is available in **English** and **Polish** so your team can choose what reads more naturally.

<img src="/images/app-przeglad.png" alt="SpeakLouder overview after sign-in: Polish main menu on the left and dashboard summary" />

## What you do in the app, in order

1. **Create a campaign** — one campaign is one follow-up project (one offer, one audience, or one funnel).
2. **Bring in leads** — depending on campaign type, you attach a lead form from ads, upload a file, or receive leads through a technical connection your team sets up.
3. **Set up the voice assistant** — instructions for incoming calls (someone calls you) and outgoing calls (the system calls the lead), plus the phone number used in the telephony provider.
4. **Build automation** — a visual flow: wait steps, calls, SMS, and optional hooks to other tools after a call.
5. **Watch leads and history** — see each person’s stage, what ran, and what is scheduled next.
