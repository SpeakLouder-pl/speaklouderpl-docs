## Process in 5 steps

1. A lead enters campaign from form, CSV, Shopify, or webhook.
2. The lead appears on the lead list.
3. Workflow decides when to call or send SMS.
4. Agent talks to lead according to your settings.
5. Lead gets result, for example qualified, no contact, rejected.

## Where to check progress

- **Leady** for person level status.
- **Automatyzacja** for scenario and timing.
- **Połączenia** for call details.
