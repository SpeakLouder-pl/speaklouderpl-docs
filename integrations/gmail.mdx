## What this integration does

Linking **Gmail** allows the product to use Google’s secure login for the features your workspace enabled (for example notifications or workflows that touch email). The exact features depend on your plan and configuration.

## How to connect

1. Open **Integrations** in the main menu.
2. Choose **Gmail** and start the connect flow.
3. In Google’s window, **accept the requested permissions**. If you skip scope or close the window early, the app may ask you to try again with full consent so a refresh token can be stored securely.

## If connection fails

Typical cases:

- **Access denied** — You closed the Google prompt or blocked permissions. Start again and accept.
- **Session refresh missing** — Google did not return a long-lived refresh token. Revoke the app in your Google account security page and connect again, accepting all prompts.

Your administrator can confirm whether backend encryption for tokens is configured in your environment.

## Privacy note

Treat connected Gmail like any business inbox: only connect accounts your organization approves, and review internal policies for customer data.
