## What this integration does

Connecting **Meta** lets SpeakLouder see the **Facebook pages and lead forms** tied to your ad account. That is how **Meta-style campaigns** know which form to use and can keep lead fields in sync with your ads.

## What you need before connecting

- A **Meta Business** setup with a **Facebook Page**.
- **Admin access** to the ad account or assets you want to use.
- You must complete Meta’s consent screens; if Meta returns an error, the app shows a short explanation (for example missing page permissions).

## Where to connect

1. Log in to [app.speaklouder.pl](https://app.speaklouder.pl).
2. Open **Integrations** in the main menu.
3. Use **Connect Meta** (or the equivalent button on the Meta card).
4. Finish the login and permission flow in the popup window.

## After connecting

- In a **dynamic Meta** campaign, open **Forms** and select the lead form that belongs to this campaign.
- If you change forms or pages in Meta, you may need to refresh or reconnect according to what the product shows.

## Privacy note

Only the permissions required for lead delivery and form listing are requested. You still control ad spend and creative inside Meta.
